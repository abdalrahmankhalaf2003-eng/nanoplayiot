⚡ SETUP GUIDE

Connect your ESP32 in 4 steps

🔧 Connect ESP32 to this leaderboard

  1. Open NanoPlay.ino in Arduino IDE
  2. Set WIFI_SSID and WIFI_PASS to your home WiFi
  3. Set PLAYER_NAME to your name (appears in leaderboard)
  4. Upload → play a game → score appears here automatically! 🌍

📦 Required Libraries

U8g2 · ArduinoJson v6 · ESP32 Arduino core
